Welcome to Lilac Valley

Lilac Valley Farm Stay is a simple, rustic farm stay accommodation in the hills of the Blue Mountains.

About

Lilac Valley Farm is owned by Marina Ye, a local interior designer who purchased the old rundown and spent years of hard work renovating the property.

Lilac Valley Farm is placed on four acres within the Blue Mountains is home to sheep, chickens, ducks and one very friendly goat named Rosie.

Lilac Valley Farm is a luxury accommodation experience for guests who are seeking a unique and relaxing getaway, in a bueatiful location.
